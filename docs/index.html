Description

For our first year at the Design and Development of videogames degree in the Project 1 subject we had to replicate some classical fighting videogames. We chose Fatal Fury: King of fighters.

Fatal Fury: King of fighters

Fatal Fury King of fighters is a fighting videogame developed and published by SNK originally in 1991 for SNK's NEO GEO arcade machine MVS and not too much later for the home console version. This game has a lot of versions and sequels but we tried to replicate the original one. For more information check out our wiki

Gameplay

Controls


                       
Controls:

Controller:
Joystick: Move and crouch
A:Kick
B:Punch
Down + Right + B: HISHO KEN (Andy's Power Wave) 
Down + left + B: ZANEI KEN (Dash)
Start: Next scene

Keyboard (not intended to be the way of playing):

ESC: Close Game

Player1 Controls:
D: Move Right
A: Move Left
W: Jump (need to be fixed)
S: Crouch
F: HISHO KEN (Andy's Power Wave) 
C: ZANEI KEN (Dash)
X: SHORYU DAN (Windmill move)
T: Punch
R: Kick

Player 2 Controls:
L: Move Right
J: Move Left
I: Jump (need to be fixed)
K: Crouch
H: HISHO KEN (Andy's Power Wave)
M: ZANEI KEN (Dash)
N: SHORYU DAN (Windmill move)
Y: Punch
U: Kick

Debug Mode:
F1: Show and hide colliders
F4: Player 1 wins round instant
F5: Turn on/off godmode



Using the following SDL libraries:
SDL: https://www.libsdl.org/
SDL_Image: https://www.libsdl.org/projects/SDL_image/
SDL_Mixer: https://www.libsdl.org/projects/SDL_mixer/
